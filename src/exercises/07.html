Good morning across the world

In a world of around 6,000 languages, there are tons of ways to greet each other and say good morning. Here's some examples:

  • 🇫🇷 Bonjour - French
  • 🇩🇪 Guten Morgen - German
  • 🇮🇹 Buon giorno - Italian
  • 🇯🇵 お早うございます - Japanese
  • 🇵🇹 Bom dia - Portuguese (Portugal)
  • 🇧🇷 Bom dia - Porguese (Brazilian)
  • 🇪🇸 Buenos días - Spanish
  • 🇹🇷 Günaydin - Turkish
Learn to say "Good morning" in many different languages.
